## FAQ

**1. Do you need access to our accounts?**  
No. You type approximate plans, seats, and monthly spend. The more accurate your inputs, the tighter the benchmark.

**2. Is this legal / compliance-safe?**  
We do not scrape your vendors. Math references the same public pricing pages your procurement team would open.

**3. Why no login?**  
Cold traffic should get value immediately. We only ask for email **after** results, if you want updates.

**4. What does Credex have to do with this?**  
If the modeled savings are large, discounted infrastructure credits may capture savings that plan changes alone cannot. Credex is surfaced only in that cohort.

**5. Can I trust the savings number?**  
It is a model, not an oracle. Treat it like an internal business case: validate against invoices, then act.
